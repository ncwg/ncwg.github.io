Northern Concurrency Working Group Meeting

13th January 2017, Teesside University, UK

This one-day meeting aims to bring together researchers, from the UK and elsewhere, who are working on the theory of concurrency and related areas.

Register

The meeting is free of charge and lunch is provided. To register, please fill in the Online Registration Form.

If you plan to attend the pay-your-own-way dinner after the meeting, please specify in the registration form whether you have any dietary requirements.

For any queries, please contact João Ferreira.

Date and Venue

The meeting will take place on Friday 13th January 2017.

Location: Executive Seminar Room D4.05, Teesside University Darlington, Vicarage Road, Darlington, DL1 1JW

Darlington is well connected and can easily be accessed by car, rail, or air. The Darlington campus is close to both Durham Tees Valley Airport and Darlington train station. It is within reach of other airports, such as Newcastle, Bradford, and Leeds. Access by car is easy via A1(M), and onsite parking is available. You can find here the directions to the campus.
